Part 1 — Executive Memo

Page 1 — The Opportunity
"[Company] has committed to becoming an AI-first organization by end of year. The AI Transformation Taskforce is building the tools and workflows. AIQ is the missing piece — ensuring our people can actually drive the transformation."

The Problem

  • AI tools are available but adoption is unstructured and ad hoc
  • No framework to assess AI readiness — for candidates or existing employees
  • No standardized expectations by role, seniority, or function
  • Risk: investing in AI infrastructure that nobody uses effectively

The Opportunity

  • Talent Density: Every hire arrives AI-fluent. Every employee levels up.
  • Competitive Edge: First company in the region with a formalized AI fluency program
  • Brand Aspiration: "AI-native" becomes reality, not aspiration
  • Multiplier Effect: AIQ makes every other AI investment (Taskforce, tools, agents) more effective

Benchmarks

  • Meta: 70%+ AI tool adoption target, "Level Up" gamification, "AI Builder" roles
  • Google: 50%+ code AI-written, AI use factored into performance reviews
  • Microsoft: AI tied to performance evaluations
  • "No comparable solution exists globally — we're building from scratch" (per COO)
Page 2 — The Framework

What is AIQ?

AIQ (AI Quotient) is a 4-level maturity model that defines what AI fluency looks like at every level of the organization:

LevelNameDefinitionTarget Roles
AIQ 1AI AwareCan use AI for basic tasksJunior staff
AIQ 2AI ProficientIntegrates AI into daily workflowsMid-level staff
AIQ 3AI BuilderBuilds AI-powered solutionsSenior/Lead + technical
AIQ 4AI StrategistDrives AI transformationDirectors+

Two Populations, One Framework

Candidates (Pre-Hire):

  • Multi-touch assessment across the hiring funnel
  • Role-specific criteria: different tests for engineers vs. PMs vs. marketers
  • AI-augmented case studies, tool proficiency tests, portfolio reviews
  • Target: every new hire has a verified AIQ score before joining

Existing Employees:

  • Baseline assessment: map the entire org to AIQ 1–4
  • Continuous cycle: Baseline → Train → Measure → Embed
  • AIQ becomes a formal competency in performance reviews
  • Training paths, hackathons, gamification, AI Champions network

The AI Stack (3-Layer Model)

LayerWhatGovernance
Foundation LLMsChatGPT, Claude, GeminiStandardized (2-3 approved)
Specialized ToolsFunction-specific AI toolsPer-vertical evaluation
Application LayerCursor, workflow toolsRole-based access
