_"[Company] has committed to becoming an AI-first organization by end of year. The AI Transformation Taskforce is building the tools and workflows. AIQ is the missing piece — ensuring our people can actually drive the transformation."_

### The Opportunity

- **Competitive Edge:** First company in the region with a formalized AI fluency program
- **Brand Aspiration:** "AI-native" becomes reality, not aspiration
- **Multiplier Effect:** AIQ makes every other AI investment (Taskforce, tools, agents) more effective

- Meta: 70%+ AI tool adoption target, "Level Up" gamification, "AI Builder" roles
- Google: 50%+ code AI-written, AI use factored into performance reviews
- Microsoft: AI tied to performance evaluations
- _"No comparable solution exists globally — we're building from scratch"_ (per COO)

## Page 2 — The Framework

### What is AIQ?
AIQ (AI Quotient) is a 4-level maturity model that defines what AI fluency looks like at every level of the organization:

| Level | Name | Definition | Target Roles |
|-------|------|-----------|-------------|
| AIQ 1 | AI Aware | Can use AI for basic tasks | Junior staff |
| AIQ 2 | AI Proficient | Integrates AI into daily workflows | Mid-level staff |
| AIQ 3 | AI Builder | Builds AI-powered solutions | Senior/Lead + technical roles |
| AIQ 4 | AI Strategist | Drives AI transformation | Directors+ |

### Two Populations, One Framework

**Candidates (Pre-Hire):**
- Multi-touch assessment across the hiring funnel
- Role-specific criteria: different tests for engineers vs. PMs vs. marketers
- AI-augmented case studies, tool proficiency tests, portfolio reviews
- Target: every new hire has a verified AIQ score before joining

**Existing Employees:**
- Baseline assessment: map the entire org to AIQ 1–4
- Continuous cycle: Baseline → Train → Measure → Embed
- AIQ becomes a formal competency in performance reviews
- Training paths, hackathons, gamification, AI Champions network

### The AI Stack (3-Layer Model)

| Layer | What | Governance |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Foundation LLMs | ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini | Standardized (2-3 approved) |
| Specialized Tools | Function-specific AI tools | Per-vertical evaluation |
| Application Layer | Cursor, workflow tools | Role-based access |
