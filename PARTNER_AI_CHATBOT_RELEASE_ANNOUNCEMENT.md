# 🚀 Partner AI Chatbot: Release Announcement

## Introduction

We're excited to announce the launch of the **Partner AI Chatbot**, a new intelligent support experience designed specifically for our merchant partners. Built on the same proven architecture as our Customer AI Chatbot, this solution brings advanced automation, faster response times, and a consistent support experience across all partner touchpoints.

---

## The Problem

Our partners have been facing significant challenges with the existing support infrastructure:

- **Limited Bot Capabilities**: The current Zendesk bot is menu-driven and lacks the intelligence to handle complex partner queries effectively
- **High Agent Workload**: Low containment rates mean agents are overwhelmed with routine inquiries, leading to:
  - Increased response times
  - Reduced capacity for complex issues
  - Higher operational costs
- **Inconsistent Experience**: Partner support experience doesn't match the modern, AI-powered experience we provide to customers
- **Lack of Automation & Analytics**: Limited automation capabilities and insufficient analytics make it difficult to measure and improve support effectiveness

These challenges impact partner satisfaction and our ability to scale support operations efficiently.

---

## The New Experience

The Partner AI Chatbot delivers a comprehensive, intelligent support experience that addresses these pain points:

### **Intelligent Query Handling**
- **Natural Language Understanding**: Partners can ask questions in natural language (English and Arabic) without navigating complex menus
- **Context-Aware Responses**: The bot understands context and provides relevant, accurate answers based on partner-specific data
- **Multi-Intent Support**: Handles queries across multiple categories:
  - **Onboarding**: Application status, required documents, store activation, country expansion
  - **Operations**: Order management, authorization, capture, refunds, cancellations
  - **Finance & Settlements**: Settlement schedules, payment status, invoices, tax documents
  - **Integrations & Technical**: API keys, webhooks, plugin setup, sandbox vs. live mode
  - **Tamara Ads**: Access, billing, performance metrics
  - **Account & Access**: Login issues, roles & permissions, profile updates

### **Seamless Integration**
- **Multi-Channel Access**: Available on Partner Portal (web + widget), partner app, and support.tamara.co
- **Smart Handoff**: Intelligent routing to specialized support teams (Onboarding Support, Partner Care) when human intervention is needed
- **Live Agent Escalation**: Smooth transition to human agents via Zendesk/Salesforce integration with full context preservation

### **Partner-Specific Features**
- **Account-Aware Responses**: Provides personalized information based on partner account status (pre-live vs. live partners)
- **Settlement & Finance Tools**: Direct access to settlement schedules, payment history, and statement downloads
- **Compliance Support**: Guided workflows for document updates, ownership transfers, and regulatory requirements
- **Integration Assistance**: Step-by-step guidance for API setup, webhook testing, and plugin installations

### **Enhanced User Experience**
- **Bilingual Support**: Full English and Arabic support with Saudi dialect
- **Rich UI Elements**: Interactive chips, carousels, deep links, and file uploads for better usability
- **Contextual Memory**: Remembers recent conversations and suggests follow-ups
- **24/7 Availability**: Always-on support with intelligent out-of-hours handling

---

## Key Results & Success Metrics

We're tracking several key metrics to measure the success of the Partner AI Chatbot:

### **Containment Rate**
- **Target**: ≥ 65% deflection for informational intents
- **Impact**: Reduces agent workload by handling routine queries automatically, allowing agents to focus on complex issues

### **Bot CSAT (Partner Bot Satisfaction)**
- **Target**: ≥ 70% satisfaction score
- **Measurement**: In-line surveys triggered at end of chat, inactivity timeout, or explicit chat closure
- **Impact**: Ensures partners have a positive experience with the AI chatbot

### **Bot Issue Resolution Rate**
- **Target**: ≥ 50% of queries resolved without human intervention
- **Impact**: Demonstrates the bot's effectiveness in solving partner problems independently

### **Additional Metrics**
- **Contact Rate Reduction**: Target 5-10% reduction in overall contact volume
- **Response Time**: Near-instant responses compared to traditional ticket-based support
- **Agent Efficiency**: Improved agent productivity through better routing and context preservation
