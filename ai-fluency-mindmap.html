02
AIQ Maturity Model

Four levels. Every role maps to a target AIQ. This is the backbone of the entire initiative.

AIQ Progression Staircase
AIQ 1
AI Aware
Draft emails, summarize, basic prompting
AIQ 2
AI Proficient
Daily workflow integration, AI-assisted analysis & creation
AIQ 3
AI Builder
Custom agents, automated workflows, AI integrations
AIQ 4
AI Strategist
Drives transformation, redesigns processes, evaluates AI investments
Role × Seniority Target Map

Technical roles shift expectations +1 level. A mid-level engineer targets AIQ 3, while a mid-level marketer targets AIQ 2.
